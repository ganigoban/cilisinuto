Channing Tatum Is the Prettiest Unicorn of All Time

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  178 words  |  Billy Koelling

This weekend, Channing Tatum proved, once and for all, that he is the most impressive unicorn in all the land. As if there was ever any doubt. Attending a pre-Halloween party with daughter Everly and wife Jenna, who were both dressed as fairies, Tatum wore the prettiest, purple unicorn costume you've ever seen. He accessorized the outfit by carrying a festive pumpkin, and wearing a rainbow tail. Needless to say, he looked flawless.
