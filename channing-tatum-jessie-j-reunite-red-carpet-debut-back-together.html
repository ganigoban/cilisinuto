Channing Tatum &amp; Jessie J Are Back Together After Split

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  358 words  |  Patria Henriques
  • Channing Tatum and Jessie J just made their red carpet debut, a month after reports they'd called it quits.
  • The couple stepped out in Los Angeles for the MusiCares Person Of The Year event, in honor of Aerosmith.
  • Singer Jessie J wore a revealing silver gown, featuring cut-outs and a major leg slit.

It looks like Channing Tatum and Jessie J are very much back on.

On Friday night, Tatum and his British musician beau made their red carpet debut as a couple in Los Angeles at MusiCares Person Of The Year. This year, the event honored Aerosmith.

Jessie J looked seriously stunning on the red carpet in a fitted silver dress. The incredible gown featured a huge leg slit, and a cutaway back section. Most importantly, the pop star looked smitten with actor Tatum, and the pair showed plenty of PDA on the red carpet.

In December 2019, a source told People of Tatum and Jessie J's split, "There wasn’t any drama. They just decided to part ways and are still good friends." The same month, it was revealed that Tatum was on dating app Raya, suggesting that he was already looking for love. According to Us Weekly, the actor's profile hilariously read, "And yes, I used to be a stripper. Sorry."

It's undeniable that Tatum and Jessie J look super happy to be back together again.
