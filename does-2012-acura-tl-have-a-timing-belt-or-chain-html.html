What is the purpose of a timing belt?

A timing belt, timing chain, or cambelt is a component of an internal combustion engine that synchronises the rotation of the crankshaft and the rotation of the camshaft(s) so that the engine’s valves open and close at the proper times during the intake and exhaust strokes of each of the engine’s cylinders.
