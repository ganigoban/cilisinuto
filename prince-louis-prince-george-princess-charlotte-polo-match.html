Prince Louis, Prince George, Princess Charlotte Attend Polo Match

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  456 words  |  Kelle Repass
  • Prince George, Princess Charlotte, and Prince Louis were seen at a polo match with Kate Middleton and Prince William today.
  • Meghan Markle and her son, Archie, were also spotted at the event.
  • William and Harry are facing off on the polo field for charity.

All three of Prince William and Kate Middleton's children—Prince George, 5, Princess Charlotte, 4, and Prince Louis, 1—are attending a polo match with their parents today, according to royal reporters. The Duke of Cambridge and his brother, Prince Harry, are playing against each other at the King Power Royal Charity Polo Day to raise funds for various organizations. Meghan Markle and her son, Archie, are also in attendance.

Duchess Kate was photographed with her youngest, Louis, on the polo grounds. The animated one-year-old wore a navy polo shirt with green shorts for the summertime occasion. Meanwhile, his mother looked chic in a pink sundress and espadrilles.

Tim Rooke/Shutterstock

Tim Rooke/Shutterstock

According to royal correspondent Rebecca English, Louis kept his mother on her toes. He was seen holding her hand, "toddling around," and running toward the horses. At one point, the Duchess of Cambridge even carried him to wave to his father.

Although George and Charlotte didn't spend as much time in front of the cameras, royal correspondent Richard Palmer added that Louis's two older siblings were at the match as well. English reported that the oldest Cambridge child was seen "holding a polo mallet" while his younger sister sported a floral dress.

Later, George and Charlotte were spotted hanging out in the back of a car while their mother was seated with Louis beside them.

Attending polo matches is nothing new for the royals; they've been seen making casual appearances with family through the years. Last summer, Kate made a sweet cameo at a game with George and Charlotte, along with Autumn Phillips (wife of Peter Phillips, Prince William's cousin), and her children, Savannah and Isla.
