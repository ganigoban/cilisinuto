Halle Berry Stuns in a Revealing Red Swimsuit with Deep V-Neck

 Posted on September 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Patria Henriques
  • Halle Berry celebrated her 54th birthday just over a week ago, and the Oscar-winning actress has been hitting the beach once again to celebrate.
  • Berry shared a photo taken with her trainer, Peter Lee Thomas, and revealed the tracks they've been working out to this summer.
  • The star looked stunning in a red one-piece swimsuit featuring a deep V-cut neckline.

Halle Berry never fails to impress us with both her style and her dedication to working out.

Having already celebrated her 54th birthday with a pantsless skateboarding photo and by wearing the chicest cut-out black bikini we've seen in a long time, Berry decided to hit the beach once again. This time, the actress was accompanied by her personal trainer, Peter Lee Thomas, as the pair reflected on the workout tracks which had kept them motivated in 2020.

For the occasion, Berry wore an incredible red one-piece swimsuit, which had hints of Baywatch about it, but also featured a deep V-cut neckline.

Berry captioned the photo, "Happy #FitnessFriday everyone!! Motivation is KEY these days, and while @peterleethomas and I will often work out without music, a good playlist can be a game changer! So today, we’re sharing our #SummerPlaylist, the songs we’ve been sweating to all summer (especially in this #HeatWave! ☀️😫). Check stories now and get to steppin’!"

Meanwhile, Berry's personal trainer celebrated the star's 54th birthday by posting a sweet photo of them together. He wrote, "There are no amount of words that can truly articulate just how thankful I am to have crossed paths with this extraordinary woman. Wishing the happiest of birthdays to my friend, training partner and spirit animal @halleberry 🦁."

Basically, this duo is total #workoutgoals.
