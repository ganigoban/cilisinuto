Can I use poly pipe for sprinkler system?

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1081 words  |  Patria Henriques

Answer 200 PSI is the maximum pressure that the typical 1″ PVC pipe used for lawn sprinkler systems is rated to endure. Poly pipe of comparable size (1″) is rated to resist 100 PSI. In fact, for polyethylene to attain the same pressure rating as PVC, the walls would need to be twice as thick as those of PVC. Squeeze clamps or saddle fittings are used with poly pipe, and they are not rated. [Read More]
