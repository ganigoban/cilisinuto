Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jeffree Star was born in Orange County, California. He developed an interest in makeup at a young age and would often experiment with his mother’s cosmetics. After high school, Star moved to Los Angeles, where he worked various makeup, modeling, and music jobs. He gained fame on MySpace, where he blogged about his life while also posting his music and fashion-oriented photos.

Music Career

Star’s music career began in 2007 when he was offered a contract by Popsicle Records. His first album, “Beauty Killer,” was released in 2009 and featured collaborations with several artists. Despite his initial success in the music industry, Star later decided to leave the music world to focus on his makeup business.

Jeffree Star Cosmetics

In 2014, Star founded Jeffree Star Cosmetics, using his life savings. The company began with a line of liquid lipsticks, which quickly gained popularity. Since then, the brand has expanded to include eyeshadow palettes, highlighters, clothing, and accessories. The success of Jeffree Star Cosmetics has significantly contributed to Star’s net worth.

YouTube Career

Star has also made a name for himself on YouTube, where he posts makeup tutorials, product reviews, and personal vlogs. His YouTube channel, boasting over 16 million subscribers, serves as a significant marketing platform for his cosmetics line and is a substantial source of income.

Real Estate Investments

Star’s wealth is not only tied to his cosmetics line and YouTube channel. He has made significant real estate investments over the years. In 2019, he purchased a $14.6 million mansion in Hidden Hills, California. He also owns a property in Grand Rapids, Michigan.

Controversies and Impact on Net Worth

Star’s career has not been without controversy. He has been involved in several public feuds with other celebrities and has faced accusations of racism and bullying. These controversies have occasionally impacted his brand’s reputation and, consequently, his net worth.
