Toni Storm Becomes 3-Time AEW Womens World Champion At Full Gear

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  254 words  |  Martina Birk

AEW brought quite a show to the Kia Forum for Full Gear. The show went a bit without a title change, but that changed eventually.

Ringside News provided full coverage of AEW Full Gear. It was quite a show, and there was no shortage of talking points throughout the event.

Toni Storm is in the middle of some great work with her Timeless character. She took on Hikaru Shida at Full Gear with the AEW Women’s Title on the line. Toni Storm really wanted that title back, and she had a lot to prove.

About halfway through the match, Toni Storm placed something in the back of her wrestling tights. Then she nailed a hip attack in the corner and won the match clean.

This marks Toni Storm’s third run as AEW Women’s World Champion. After the match was over, they cut to a black and white celebration for her Hollywood homecoming. Mariah May came down with flowers as well, and Toni had a lot of celebrating to do.
