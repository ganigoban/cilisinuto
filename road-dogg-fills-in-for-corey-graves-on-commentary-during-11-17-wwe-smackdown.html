Road Dogg Fills In For Corey Graves On Commentary During 11/17 WWE SmackDown

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  251 words  |  Patria Henriques

This is a happy time for WWE, because their family grew by one last week. Because of that, SmackDown had to shake things up a bit.

Ringside News has comprehensive coverage of WWE SmackDown’s November 17th episode. It was quite a show, and it also started off with a bit of a surprise.

Kevin Owens took over for Corey Graves last week, as Graves is off due to his paternity leave. Carmella gave birth to their son, Dimitri Paul Polinsky.

This week on SmackDown, Road Dogg was on commentary. He had a bit of fun with his introduction, as he said that Graves and Wade Barrett had their kid. Only time will tell how things pan out for him this week.

Kevin Owens was actually suspended last week as he hit Austin Theory and Grayson Waller when he wasn’t supposed to. Now, KO is out of action, but it seems that they were able to fill in for Corey Graves again this week.
