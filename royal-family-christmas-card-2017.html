Royal Family Christmas Card 2017

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Tandra Barner

This morning, Kensington Palace unveiled the photo Prince William and Kate Middleton are using on their Christmas card. The picture-perfect shot features the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge with their children, Prince George, 4, and Princess Charlotte, 2, on palace grounds.

The family of four avoid a festive red-and-white color scheme in favor of matching blue outfits for the portrait, which was taken earlier this year by royal photographer Chris Jackson. It appears the Duchess, who is currently pregnant with her and the Duke's third child, wasn't yet showing her baby bump when the photo was shot.

In addition to revealing their annual Christmas card photo, the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge also announced where Princess Charlotte will be going to nursery school. The two-year-old will attend Willcocks Nursery School in London starting in January 2018. The school is "delighted" to accept the Princess as a new student. "We look forward to welcoming Charlotte to our nursery in January," it said in a statement.
