Emily Ratajkowski Shows Off Red Hair in Plunging Black Swimsuit

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  305 words  |  Kelle Repass

Emily Ratajkowski debuted her red summer hair on TikTok yesterday, and hours later, showed off the fiery look on Instagram, wearing a barely-there swimsuit.

In an Instagram slideshow, the model poses on her green velvet couch (remember the "bisexual green couch” trend she kick-started some months ago?) wearing nothing but a plunging black one-piece suit, with rosy makeup, a glossy pouty lip, and her warm red hair styled in a straight, messy ’do.

In the first photo, she leans up close to the camera; in the second, she leans back on the sofa, displaying her shiny black suit and a generous dose of sideboob. The rest of the sultry photos have her posing in different angles on the couch.

“Gone red,” she wrote in the post caption, thanking the team that made her hair transformation possible.

Her post came after she shared a TikTok video of herself dancing inside a New York City subway car while wearing a little black strapless dress and showing off her new hair. She finished the casual outfit with white sneakers with peach-colored soles and a green crochet tote bag by Prada. Her makeup was bronzy and included a pouty, glossy lip.

“Not a filter,” she wrote over the clip, referring to her hair. (The fake redhead filter on TikTok has been trending for some time, but EmRata’s hair is the real thing.)

“Red for the summer,” she added. “Soemtimes [sic] ya just need a change you kno?”
